About Wildlife Tracker

Wildlife Tracker is a platform for USC students and researchers to record, view, and explore real-time wildlife sightings around campus. Our goal is to build a community-powered map of local biodiversity.

Project Goals

  • Create a centralized wildlife sighting database
  • Support research and field studies
  • Improve campus community engagement
  • Visualize wildlife data on an interactive live map
